My top 3 favorite movies!

A list of Paris' absolute favorite films that just happen to all be made by Studio Ghibli.

Howl's Moving Castle

This is my absolute favorite movie of all time. It has stunning landscapes, loveable characters, and the coziest atmostphere imagineable.

Spirited away

This is my number 2. It has funky characters and spunk paired with the famous Ghibli coziness.

Ponyo

Last, but definitely not least is Ponyo. It is an absolutely adorable film with cute characters and lots of great energy.
